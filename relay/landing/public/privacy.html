Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

The short version: Much Better Authenticator collects nothing. Your 2FA secrets stay on your phone, the relay sees only encrypted bytes, and nothing is shared with anyone.

What the app does

Much Better Authenticator generates two-factor authentication (2FA) codes for your online accounts. The app runs on your iPhone. An optional Chrome extension lets you receive codes in your browser by routing requests through a relay server we operate.

What we collect

Nothing. There are no analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporters, and no account creation. We do not know who uses the app or how often.

What stays on your device

  • Your 2FA secrets (the long codes you scan from a QR)
  • Your account labels (for example, "Google — you@example.com")
  • Biometric authentication preferences
  • iCloud Keychain sync settings

Secrets are stored in the iOS Keychain, encrypted with hardware-backed keys. The keyboard extension reads them locally and never has network access.

What goes through the relay

When you click a 2FA code in the Chrome extension, the relay forwards encrypted messages between your phone and your browser. The relay cannot decrypt any of these messages. It sees only ciphertext.

The encryption key is derived during pairing using X25519 Diffie-Hellman, with ChaCha20-Poly1305 for message encryption. The key is generated on each device and never transmitted to the relay.

What goes through Apple Push Notifications

When your iPhone is locked or backgrounded, the relay sends a wake notification through Apple Push Notification Service (APNs). The notification contains only "approve 2FA request" and a room identifier. It does not include account names, codes, or which website is requesting the code.

iCloud Keychain

If you enable iCloud Keychain sync, your 2FA secrets sync to your other Apple devices via Apple's iCloud Keychain. This sync uses Apple's end-to-end encryption, protected by your Apple ID and device passcode. We do not have access to this data.

Third parties

We do not share data with anyone. There are no advertising networks, no analytics SDKs, and no data brokers.

Data retention

The relay holds pairing rooms in memory for thirty minutes after the last activity, then evicts them. There is no database. Messages in transit are not logged.

Children

The app is not directed at children under thirteen. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under thirteen.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a material way, the "Last updated" date at the top will change. Older versions are preserved in the project's git history.
