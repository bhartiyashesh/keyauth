Quick start

  1. Install Much Better Authenticator on your iPhone and allow notifications when prompted.
  2. Open the gear in the top right, then "How to enable the keyboard." Follow the four steps to add the keyboard in iOS Settings.
  3. Add 2FA accounts by tapping the plus button. Scan a QR code from the service that's giving it to you, or enter the secret manually.
  4. Install the Chrome extension from the Chrome Web Store, then pair it with your phone using the QR code shown in the extension.

How the keyboard works

The keyboard sits above any iOS keyboard and shows your active 2FA codes. Tap a code to insert it at the cursor. The keyboard reads codes from the iPhone's secure storage and never accesses the network.

Full Access is not required. The keyboard works without it.

Troubleshooting

The keyboard does not appear on iOS

Go to Settings > General > Keyboard > Keyboards and confirm "Much Better Authenticator" is listed. If not, tap "Add New Keyboard" and select it. Then, when typing anywhere, long-press the globe icon at the bottom of the keyboard and switch to Much Better Authenticator.

The Chrome extension says "waiting for approval"

Open the Much Better Authenticator iPhone app. Check the dot next to the link icon in the top-left of the home screen:

  • Green: connected, you should see the approval sheet appear.
  • Orange: connecting. Wait a few seconds.
  • Red: disconnected. Force-quit and reopen the app.

Also check that notifications are allowed in Settings > Notifications > Much Better Authenticator. Without notification permission, the relay cannot wake the app when your phone is locked.

Codes don't match what the site expects

2FA codes are tied to a precise secret. If you re-scanned the QR or migrated from a different authenticator, make sure the secret matches what the service has on file. The clock on your iPhone also matters: if it drifts from the real time, codes will be wrong. Confirm Settings > General > Date & Time > Set Automatically is on.

I lost my phone

If you had iCloud Keychain sync turned on in the app (Settings > Sync with iCloud Keychain), your accounts restore automatically when you sign into iCloud on a new device and reinstall Much Better Authenticator.

If sync was off, you'll need to re-add each account manually using its recovery codes or by re-pairing in each service's security settings.

I want to move from Google Authenticator

Open Google Authenticator and tap the menu, then "Transfer accounts." Generate a QR code (or several, for large libraries). In Much Better Authenticator, tap the plus button and scan each QR. The import sheet lets you confirm which accounts to bring over.
